Allium is a Inventory Monitoring System for the allium production and stocks in every region of the country. It is implemented to monitor the areas and its estimated harvest, stocks of different kinds of allium, and to list the storages of allium and show the location in the map. The user can generate reports of production and stocks into excel format.

Used technologies to build this is HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, and JQuery for front-end; PHP and Laravel for back-end, Visual Studio for IDE, Adobe Photoshop for icon design and background, MySql for Database.
