Tech Aid is a Tech Assistance Management System aims to help the Bureau of Plant Industry to manage the tech support request of their employees. The employees will request support to the IT Department using the app and the IT employees will be notified. The filling up of request form is digitalized so that the use of paper and ink will be lessen. The request forms can be signed by the chiefs of offices digitaly by a click of an button. Also the IT Department can monitor received requests and generate a report.

Used technologies to build this is HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, and JQuery for front-end; PHP and Laravel for back-end, Visual Studio for IDE, Adobe Photoshop for icon design and background, MySql for Database.
